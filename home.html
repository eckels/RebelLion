RebelLioN is a Vainglory guild founded on friendship that quickly developed into a powerful eSports Organization. Almost all members play Vainglory at the competitive level, with our teams advancing very far in tournaments and qualifying for Live Final events. Aside from great sportmanship and skill, we also pride ourselves on our rebellious nature. We not only strive to shatter the Vain of our opponents, but we expect to do so with a certain swag and humour. Look for us in the fold, expect no quarter, but do expect a good time!
